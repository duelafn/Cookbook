
\begin{recipe}{Sweet Soy Glaze}%\tag{}
  \rating    3
  \nutrition 1
  \health    3
  \workload{medium}
  \time{30}
  \yield{6~\T}
  \servings{}
  \source{Internet}
  \maketitle
  % \photo{}

  \begin{ingredients2}
    2 clove & garlic\\
    \half inch & ginger\\
    2 \t & sesame oil\\
    \fourth \cup & light soy sauce\\
    3 \T & honey\\
    3 \T & brown sugar\\
    1 \T & fish sauce\\
    1 \T & rice vinegar
  \end{ingredients2}

  Mince garlic and ginger. Cook in sesame oil until fragrant (30 sec). Add
  rest of ingredients. Reduce in volume to half and let cool (thickens
  while it cools).

  \section{Or, better, just use Sunny's Korean Teriyaki with a bit of corn starch}
\end{recipe}
