
\begin{recipe}{Brownies}
  % \tag{}
  \rating    5
  \nutrition 2
  \health    2
  \workload{medium}
  \time{60}
  \yield{one $9\inch\times13\inch$ pan}
  \servings{12}
  \source{\Joy}
  % \photo{}
  \maketitle

  \begin{ingredients2}% 951g
    8 \T & butter\\% 113g: 92g fat
    4 \oz & bakers chocolate\\% 113g
    4 & eggs\\% 200g: 20g fat
    \fourth \t & salt\\
    2 \cups & sugar\\% 400g sugar
    1 \t & vanilla\\
    1 \cup & flour% 125g
  \end{ingredients2}

  Melt and \emph{cool} the butter and chocolate (carefully in microwave or
  in double boiler). beat eggs and salt foamy. Beat in sugar and vanilla.
  Add chocolate mixture. Stir in flour with a few swift strokes. Stir in
  chocolate chips. Bake 35--45 minutes at \tempF[210]{350}.
