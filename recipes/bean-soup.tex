
\begin{recipe}{Bean Soup}\tag{vegetarian}\tag{soup}
  \rating    4
  \nutrition 4
  \health    3
  \workload{light}
  \time{60}
  \yield{6 quarts}
  \servings{6}
  \source{Dean Serenevy}
  % \photo{}
  \maketitle

  \begin{ingredients2}
    1 \lb & bacon\\
    3 cans & Sylvia's Black-Eyed Peas\\
    2 \c & chicken stock\\
    2 \c & water (or dashi)\\
    1 \c & barley (dry)\\
    \half \T & black pepper\\
    \quarter \t & crushed red pepper\\
    2 & bay leaves
  \end{ingredients2}

  Cook bacon, add rest of ingredients and cook for 20--30 minutes. Blend a portion of
  the soup (a fourth or so) in a blender or food processor.

  Sylvia's Black-Eyed Peas are getting hard to find, if you make
  \reciperef{Southern Beans} from scratch, they will be best the day after.
\end{recipe}
